Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide you with the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filtering by date or category. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers. Any external services used (such as hosting providers) are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on the site. We are committed to addressing any concerns promptly.
